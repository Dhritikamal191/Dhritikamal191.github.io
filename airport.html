Airport Traffic Forecasting & Analytics Platform

An end-to-end Machine Learning and Analytics solution designed to forecast airport traffic, monitor operational performance, analyze traffic patterns, and provide explainable AI insights through an interactive Streamlit dashboard.

Executive Summary

This project presents an end-to-end Airport Traffic Forecasting and Monitoring Dashboard designed to predict future flight traffic patterns and support data-driven operational decision-making.

Historical airport traffic data was leveraged to build and evaluate machine learning models capable of forecasting total flight volumes. A time-based validation approach was implemented to simulate real-world forecasting scenarios and ensure reliable performance on unseen data.

The dashboard integrates interactive analytics, including traffic insights, scenario analysis, forecasting, model monitoring, and drift detection. SHAP-based explainability was incorporated to identify the factors that most strongly influence traffic predictions, improving model transparency and interpretability.

By transforming raw aviation data into actionable insights, the solution enables stakeholders to understand seasonal traffic trends, identify operational patterns, monitor model stability, and make informed planning decisions. The project demonstrates expertise in machine learning, explainable AI, forecasting, and the development of interactive analytical dashboards.

Project Overview

Airport traffic forecasting plays a critical role in resource planning, air traffic management, staffing, runway utilization, and operational efficiency. This project leverages machine learning to predict total airport flight traffic while providing advanced analytics, forecasting, monitoring, and explainable AI capabilities.

Business Problem

Airport authorities require accurate traffic forecasts to optimize operational planning and anticipate changes in flight demand. Traditional reporting methods often lack predictive capabilities and explainability. The objective of this project was to build a scalable analytics platform capable of forecasting airport traffic and generating actionable insights.

Explainable AI

SHAP (SHapley Additive exPlanations) was implemented to improve model transparency. Global feature importance, local prediction explanations, dependence analysis, and waterfall visualizations were incorporated into the dashboard to explain the drivers behind traffic predictions.

Forecasting & Monitoring

The platform includes a six-month forecasting module and operational monitoring dashboard. Drift metrics were implemented to track changes in traffic patterns and IFR operations, enabling continuous model performance assessment.

Key Insights

  • Traffic volume exhibits clear monthly seasonality.
  • Flight arrivals and departures are primary traffic drivers.
  • IFR operations strongly correlate with total flight traffic.
  • Certain airports consistently dominate traffic volume.
  • Traffic patterns remain stable across most operational segments.

Business Impact

The solution enables airport stakeholders to forecast future traffic demand, identify operational trends, evaluate airport performance, and improve resource planning through data-driven decision-making.
